🔒 Your Data Never Leaves Your Device

FIREtrack runs entirely in your browser — no accounts, no servers, no cloud sync. Everything you enter is stored only in your browser's local storage and is never transmitted anywhere.

✓ No account or login required
✓ No data sent to any server
✓ No ads, no tracking, no analytics
✓ No API keys or credentials stored
✓ Open to inspect — single HTML file
✓ Security headers on every request
💡 Safer than Mint, Personal Capital, or any app that requires an account — those services store your financial data on their servers. FIREtrack stores nothing anywhere except your own device.

📋 For educational & planning purposes only — not financial advice. FIREtrack uses simplified models with constant returns, no market volatility, and approximate tax estimates. Real outcomes will differ. Always consult a licensed financial advisor, CPA, or tax professional before making retirement or investment decisions.
